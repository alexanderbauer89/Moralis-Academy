What do we mean with unbundling and bundling in fintech?

It is the process of banks creating regulatory technologies.
It is the process where products in banks are being broken out and re-created by a fintech and offered as a single service and then been put back together with other products into a package of products in a bank or fintech again.
It is the process of rebuilding a bank without taking away any of the existing services.
It is the process that makes fintechs more similar to banks.
