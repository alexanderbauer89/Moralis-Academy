What main events happened during the 2nd phase in the evolution of Fintech?

Swift Network was built, Nasdaq was built, the handheld calculator was built, the first ATMs were created, and the first instances of online banking were seen.
The first high-frequency trading firms were established, and the first anti-money laundering regulations came into force.
The first digital currencies were released, and central banks got their first computers.
Distrust of banks grew to record high numbers among young people, and there was a lot of unemployment in the financial service industry.
