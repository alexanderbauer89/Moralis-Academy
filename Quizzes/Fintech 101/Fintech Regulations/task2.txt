What is the Travel Rule?

It is a regulation by the Financial Action Task Force that applies to Virtual Asset Service Providers
It is a regulation that forces banks to offer crypto wallet services to their customers.
It is a regulation that forces anyone holding cryptocurrencies to declare their holdings when traveling in and out of any country in the world.
It is a Regulation that forces any bank to track its customers wherever and whenever they travel.
