What are Stash keys?

Public/private keypairs used to define a stash account. The account is like a savings account
Public/private keypairs used to define a controller account and is used to set the preferences of the stash account
Public/private keypairs used to define a stash account and is used to set preferences of the stash account
Public/private keypairs used to define a stash account and is used to spend the funds of the controller account
